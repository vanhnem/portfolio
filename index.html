About me


Hi! My name is Vanhnem Darkhuah, and I am currently a senior at Perry Meridian High School. A long-term goal of mine is to pursue a technology career and contribute to philanthropic purposes. Thus far, to challenge myself with our school's curriculum, I have taken a variety of AP courses: AP Computer Science A, AP Physics 1: Algebra-based, AP Seminar, AP US History, AP Literature and Composition, and AP World History, and I plan to take more this upcoming year. In relation to technology, I have taken all of the Computer Science classes available at my school and found myself enjoying creating projects and bringing my ideas to life.

As a student who has taken French I-IV and plans on taking AP French next year, I've been committed to our school's/district's French program for the past four years. I have also been a Representative for French Honor Society for two consecutive years, where I am tasked with the responsibility of handling the club's volunteer opportunities.

Additionally, I have been a Key Club member since my sophomore year, and became a Junior Director my junior year. As a director, I organized school/community service events, logged hours on a record sheet, and stayed consistent with my participation in the organization. Next year, I will be an Editor for the EC (Executive Committee) next year. Volunteering has been a significant part of my high school career and I plan to commit time to volunteering until I physically can't.
